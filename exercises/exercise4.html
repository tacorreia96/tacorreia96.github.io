Angela's Cakes

Angela Corriea has been making and designing cakes for over 16 years now. starting off by offering up her skills to family events like birthday parties, weddings, and holidays, Angela has since become a bit of a master cake decorator. She has been a lead cake designer at three bakeries in Massachusetts. Now, for the first time, her skills and artistic abilities are available to the general public. She only has a personal instagram where she sometimes features her cake creations, but this website would allow for an "about me" page, a portfolio of all her cake creations, a pricing page, and a contact page.
